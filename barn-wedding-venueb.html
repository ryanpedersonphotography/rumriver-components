About Our Venue

Where Rustic Charm Meets Modern Elegance

Nestled in the heart of California's wine country, Willow Creek Barn is a meticulously restored 1890s barn that seamlessly blends historic character with contemporary luxury.

Our 40-acre estate provides breathtaking backdrops for your special day, from rolling meadows to ancient oak groves, creating the perfect canvas for your love story.
