Your Dream Barn Wedding Awaits

Willow Creek Barn is a restored 1890s barn that combines rustic charm with modern amenities. Set on 40 acres of pristine countryside, our venue offers the perfect backdrop for your special day.

From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations, we provide an enchanting setting where memories are made and love stories unfold against the backdrop of weathered wood, twinkling lights, and natural beauty.

  • Historic barn with original architecture
  • Accommodates up to 250 guests
  • Climate-controlled comfort year-round
  • Stunning outdoor ceremony sites
  • Professional wedding coordination
  • Ample parking and accessibility
